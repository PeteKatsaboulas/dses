Our Mission

Deep South Elite Sports will provide proper guidance and a high quality program in Mississippi for athletes ages 7-18. The program will help develop the individual skills needed to play high school, collegiate, and professional baseball. Deep South Elite Sports will respond to the needs of the developing athletes, the community, and underprivileged children by teaching discipline, sportsmanship, pride, ethics, and integrity. We will help raise much-needed funds and awareness for many local charities. Deep South Elite Sports also provides funding to purchase sports uniforms, equipment, team fees, facility use, and professional instruction.
